— About Us —

Step into the inviting ambiance of Sunarga, where the fusion of traditional flavors and contemporary dining experience awaits. Our extensive menu offers a tantalizing array of authentic Indian dishes, meticulously crafted to satisfy every palate. From aromatic curries to succulent tandoori delights, each dish is prepared with the finest ingredients and spices, ensuring an unforgettable culinary journey.

With two floors of spacious seating, our restaurant offers ample room for guests to dine and unwind in comfort. Whether you prefer an intimate dinner for two or a gathering with friends, our versatile space accommodates all occasions.

Enjoy the convenience of our well-stocked bar, relaxing atmosphere and cheerful service.
